How To Play

» You can play the game with your friend or with any random player.

» Player1: If you want to play with your friend then create a game and tick that invite-only checkbox.

» A room-ID will be displayed on your screen, share that with your friend.

» If you want to play with any random player then select "Join random game" button.
After selecting the button, a dropdown menu will be visible. Select any room from there.

» If you want to join a game with your friend, then select "Join with a friend".
